# Slide switch

![Slide switch](../img/slide-switch.png)

2-position SPDT switch: the common toggles between two terminals.

## Pins

| Pin | Role |
|--------|------|
| **1** | Position A |
| **2** | Common |
| **3** | Position B |

## Usage

- Common (2) to the pin; one position to ground, the other to +.
- Click to toggle in simulation.
