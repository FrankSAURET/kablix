# DIP switch ×8

![DIP switch ×8](../img/dip-switch.png)

Block of 8 independent micro-switches (configuration / addresses).

## Pins

| Pin | Role |
|--------|------|
| **1a–8a** | Side A of each switch |
| **1b–8b** | Corresponding side B |

## Usage

- Each switch links its side a to its side b when closed.
- Often: one side to ground, the other in `INPUT_PULLUP`.
