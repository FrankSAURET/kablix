# LED bar graph

![LED bar graph](../img/led-bar.png)

Bar graph of 10 independent LEDs (anodes A1–A10, cathodes C1–C10).

## Pins

| Pin | Role |
|--------|------|
| **A1–A10** | Anodes of the 10 LEDs |
| **C1–C10** | Cathodes of the 10 LEDs |

## Properties

| Property | Role | Default |
|-----------|------|--------|
| `color` | Color (GYR = green/yellow/red, or solid) | GYR |

## Usage

- One resistor per LED used.
- Handy for a VU-meter / level gauge.
