# Breadboard

![Breadboard](../img/breadboard.png)

Solderless prototyping board. The holes are linked in strips to connect
components by simply plugging in their legs.

## Internal connections

- **Power rails** (top and bottom rows, **+** red and **–** blue): all the holes of a rail are linked along its whole length.
- **Center columns**: the 5 holes of a same column (a–e, then f–j) are linked together.
- The **center groove** separates the two halves (a–e and f–j): handy for integrated circuits.

## Properties

| Property | Role | Default |
|-----------|------|--------|
| `size` | Size (mini / medium / full) | medium |

## Usage

- Place the power supply on the + / – rails, the components on the columns.
- Component legs **snap** automatically onto the 10 px grid.
